CHARACTERS

Baron

The few young adventurers that the Adventure Guild has always find themselves tangled up in baffling bouts of bad luck. He is the only active member of his own adventure group, known as "We Are Group" after all the other members decided to "take leave" following a series of unfortunate incidents.

Aoki

An exorcist who roams the land with Li as his base of operations, evil spirits fleeing wherever he goes. As the heir to a clan of exorcists, he has always possessed abilities superior to most.
